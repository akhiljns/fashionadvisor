Fashion Event Judgement

Fashion is a popular style or practice, especially in clothing,footwear,accessories,makeup,hairstyle and body. Fashion is a distinctive and often constant trend in the style in which a person dresses. It is the prevailing styles in behaviour and the newest creations of textile designers.Because the more technical term costume is regularly linked to the term "fashion", the use of the former has been relegated to special senses like fancy or masquerade, while "fashion" generally means clothing, including the study of it. Although aspects of fashion can be feminine or masculine, some trends are androgynous.

Press releases, events, photo series, influencers and sponsorships are all powerful public relations tools with their own individual uses and strong points. Sponsorships are amongst the most costly investments in their public image a luxury brand can make. But if the outlet is selected correctly, the impact on public opinion can be powerful and long-lasting.For sponsors, fashion weeks are brand-building platforms.More sponsors means bigger budgets.
